The Calculator is a powerful tool that assists in studying by simplifying complex mathematical problems. Whether it's solving algebraic equations, performing quick arithmetic calculations, or graphing functions, a calculator can save time and increase accuracy. It allows students to focus on understanding mathematical concepts rather than getting bogged down in manual calculations. Additionally, using a calculator regularly builds familiarity with mathematical operations and enhances problem-solving skills. For those who need extra help, the Ask AI Math Assistant is available to provide support on a wide range of math topics. From basic arithmetic to advanced calculus, the assistant can guide students through problems step-by-step, offering explanations and alternative approaches to understanding math. With the regular use of both tools, students can improve their math skills and boost their confidence in tackling even the toughest assignments.


This project is designed to assist students and professionals by providing an easy way to access computational resources. It is developed using the following technologies:

1. Python: The core programming language for building the application.
2. Flask: A lightweight web framework used to develop the web interface and manage routing.
3. WolframAlpha API: Integrated for advanced computational capabilities, solving mathematical problems and providing scientific data.
